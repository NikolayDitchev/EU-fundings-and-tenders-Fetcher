Description: 
Scope:This call for proposals aims to strengthen consumer protection by increasing access to debt advice services for consumers in EU Member States and ensure that relevant services can be improved. The main objectives include raising awareness among stakeholders about the importance of debt advice and enhancing the effectiveness of stable debt advice structures and networks. The initiative aims to benefit consumers with debt-related challenges, by supporting experts who offer relevant services at local, national and European level.
