Description: 
ExpectedOutcome:Projects under Topic 1 can address different educational sectors or bridge educational sectors, and must support high quality and inclusive digital education, in line with the Digital Education Action Plan 2021-2027.Proposals submitted under this topic must address one of the following priorities:Priority 1: Building a teaching framework and policy intervention aiming to boost gender balance in pursuing ICT related studies and professionsPriority 2: Digital well-being: Putting into practice what worksPriority 3: Data literacy strategies in primary and secondary education
