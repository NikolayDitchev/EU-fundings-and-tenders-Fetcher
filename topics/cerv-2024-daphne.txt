Description: 
Scope:With this call, diveded into 4 priorities, we aim at financing projects that will:support the development of large-scale, integrated actions to combat gender-based violence and achieve long-term and structural changes with a wide geographical coverageprotect and support victims and survivors of gender-based violence, including childrenprevent gender-based violence, in the domestic sphere, in intimate relationships, and online, including through targeted actions with perpetrators  make integrated child protection systems work in practice
